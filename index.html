About Canada

Canada is a country located in North America, bordered by the United States to the south and the Arctic Circle to the north.
It is the second-largest country in the world by total area, and it has a diverse landscape ranging from forests and mountains to lakes and prairies.
English and French are the official languages of Canada.

Provinces of Canada

Canada is divided into ten provinces and three territories. Each province has its own unique culture and attractions:

  • British Columbia (BC) is known for its stunning natural beauty, with mountains, forests, and a long coastline.
  • Alberta is home to the Rocky Mountains, as well as the city of Calgary, which hosts the annual Calgary Stampede.
  • Saskatchewan is a prairie province with a rich agricultural history.
  • Manitoba is known for its lakes and forests, as well as the city of Winnipeg, which has a vibrant arts scene.
  • Ontario is home to the capital city of Ottawa, as well as Toronto, which is the largest city in Canada.
  • Quebec is the only predominantly French-speaking province in Canada, and it is known for its rich history and culture.
  • New Brunswick is a maritime province with a long coastline and a mix of English and French influences.
  • Nova Scotia is also a maritime province, known for its seafood and beautiful coastlines.
  • Prince Edward Island (PEI) is a small island province with a picturesque landscape and a strong agricultural tradition.
  • Newfoundland and Labrador is a province made up of the island of Newfoundland and the mainland region of Labrador. It is known for its rugged coastline and rich history.

Attractions in Canada

There are many amazing things to see and do in Canada. Some of the top attractions include:

  • The Niagara Falls, a stunning natural wonder located on the border between Ontario and New York State
  • Banff National Park, a beautiful mountain park in Alberta
  • The CN Tower, a iconic landmark in Toronto
  • The Old Quebec City, a historic neighborhood with cobblestone streets and charming architecture
